Welcome to CapitalCompass

CapitalCompass is your personal finance management tool designed to help you keep track of your accounts, manage your budgets, and gain better control over your finances. With a user-friendly interface and powerful features, you can easily manage all aspects of your financial life.

Key Features

  • Accounts Management: Easily manage all your financial accounts, including Checking, Savings, and Cash accounts. View balances, edit account details, and delete accounts as needed.
  • Budget Tracking: Create and monitor budgets to ensure you are staying on track with your financial goals. Allocate funds to different categories and track your spending.
  • Transaction Management: Record transactions, categorize expenses, and view transaction history for detailed insights into your spending habits.
  • Analytics and Reports: Gain insights into your financial health with detailed analytics and reports. Visualize your income, expenses, and savings to make informed financial decisions.
  • Security and Privacy: Your data is securely stored and protected, ensuring your financial information remains confidential.

Getting Started

To start using CapitalCompass, you can create a new budget. You can choose between two types of budgets based on your needs:

  • Fixed Budget: This is ideal for users with consistent income and expenses. You set fixed amounts for each category, and the app helps you track your spending against these limits.
  • Flexible Budget: Suitable for those with variable income or expenses, allowing for dynamic adjustments to your budget categories based on your financial situation.

You can add your recurring accounts under the "Accounts" section. This includes setting up different types of accounts like Checking, Savings, and Cash accounts.

You can add inflow and outflow transactions using the "Transactions" section, helping you to manage and categorize your income and expenses.

For a deeper understanding of your finances, you can view financial analysis through detailed analytics and reports. These tools provide insights into your income, expenses, savings, and investments.
